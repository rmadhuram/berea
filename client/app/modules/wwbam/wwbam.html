WHO WANTS TO BE A BIBLE MILLIONAIRE?

Christ Church of India, Harvest Festival 2019

HOW TO PLAY?

  • Enter your name in the sign-up sheet to play.
  • Pay $3 to play one game.
  • Maximum of 2 consecutive games per person.

RULES

  • There are 5 questions (levels) from the Bible per game.
  • Each question has 4 choices. Select the correct choice.
  • You have 30 seconds to answer a question.
  • It gets harder by the level!
  • You can go to next level only if you correctly answer current level.
  • You may use your print Bibles
  • No internet search allowed :) !!
  • Win prizes if you get past 3rd level!!
